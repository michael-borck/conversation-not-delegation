Strong RTCF Prompt

We have a small team, limited budget, and need to differentiate from established competitors like Jira and Asana. Research Analysis RTCF Prompt
You are an experienced higher education analyst. Identify the three strongest themes and two areas of concern in this student feedback data. This is from a second-year undergraduate unit with 85 responses across two campuses. Present findings as a summary paragraph followed by a table with columns: Theme, Evidence, and Recommended Action.
